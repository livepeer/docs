# Welcome to the Livepeer Docs

Explore guides and a variety of resources to help you get started adding live
and on demand video experiences to your application using the open and
decentralized Livepeer Protocol. Interested in participating in the network as
an orchestrator or delegator? We got you covered too.

Join the [Discord
community](https://discord.gg/livepeer) for help along the way.
