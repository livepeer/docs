If you're exploring the possibility of joining the AI Video Subnet as an
_Gateway_ to perform **AI inference task routing** on the Livepeer network
provide inference services to customers, this guide is for you. It's tailored to
help you navigate the setup process of an AI Gateway, mirroring the
[Orchestrator Setup Guide](/gateways/guides/overview) for the _Mainnet
Transcoding Network_, but with adjustments for AI operations. Dive into the
subpages for a step-by-step walkthrough. For a deeper understanding of
orchestrator operations, the
[Orchestrator Setup Guide](/gateways/guides/overview) is your go-to resource.

## Prerequisites

Before you start seting up your _AI Gateway_, ensure you have:

- A [Linux](https://ubuntu.com/download/desktop) system (Support for Windows and
  MacOS coming soon)
- Root user access to the system
