## Core concepts

Discover the 3 core pillars of Flatfile: Spaces, Workbooks, and Events. Experience their seamless efficiency and collaboration as they power your data workflow in Flatfile.

<CardGroup cols={1}>
  <Card title="Spaces" href="./concepts/spaces" icon="circle-1">
    Where the workflow unfolds, encompassing user invitations, data loading, and
    data egress.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Workbooks" href="./concepts/workbooks" icon="circle-2">
    The foundation for data normalization, encompassing mapping, transformation,
    validation, and collaboration throughout the entire process.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Events" href="./concepts/events" icon="circle-3">
    The mechanism for orchestrating the workflow, enabling the execution of bulk
    tasks and automation of various aspects of the experience.
  </Card>
</CardGroup>
